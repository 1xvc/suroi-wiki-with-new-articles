The **Washing Machine** is an [Obstacle](/obstacles) added in the v0.9.0 "Behind Closed Doors" update on July 30th, 2023. It is explosive and drops no loot.

# Location & Spawning
Washing Machines spawn in the Garages of [Houses](/buildings/house).

# Loot & More
The Washing Machine reflects bullets, and has either the "Verified or "no kil pls" skin as [loot](/loot#washing_machine).

# Tips
- The Washing Machine doesn't make effective cover due to it's positioning.
- If you want to loot the Washing Machine, do it last since it has the least important loot in the House.
