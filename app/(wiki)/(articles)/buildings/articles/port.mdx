The **Port** is a very large structure added in the v0.11.0 "Making Waves" update on October 22nd, 2023. It has a large number of buildings and containers, and is also the home of the [Ship](/buildings/ship).

# Location & Spawning
One Port atempts to spawn per map, always at the edge of the map.
    - Very rarely, a Port can fail to spawn on a map.

# Areas
## Ship
Main article: [Port Shed](/buildings/port_shed)
On the outer edge of the Port, the Ship spawns in the water.

## Port Warehouses
Main article: [Port Warehouse](/buildings/port_warehouse)

## Port Shed
Main article: [Port Shed](/buildings/port_shed)
A Port Shed spawns towards the center of the Port, alongside a lowered boom barrier.

## Crane
Main article: [Crane](/buildings/crane)
A crane always spawns parallel to the Ship, and contains a large amount of loot underneath it.

## Containers
The Port contains a large number of Containers in various locations. They are useful for getting some basic weapons, armor, and healing items to engage other players.

# Tips
- Be careful, as their are many players at the port vying for loot.

# Trivia
- The Port was designed by platonthek
