The **Glock 19**, known in-game as the G19, is a 9mm semi-automatic pistol. It was added to the game in the v0.4.0 "Supplies received" update on June 17th, 2023.

# Background
The Glock 19 is a "Compact" version of the Glock 17. It carries fifteen rounds in comparison to the Glock 17's seventeen rounds. The Glock series was the first commerically successful polymer framed pistol. It is famed for its reliabilty and low cost. Glock variants are in use all over the globe in police, military, and other security forces.
