The **Beretta ARX160**, known in-game as the **ARX-160** or simply the **ARX** is an assault rifle utilizing 5.56mm ammunition.
# Background
The **Beretta ARX160** was developed for the Italian Armed Forces in 2008. It is available in both 7.62x39 and 5.56x45 NATO. The ARX160 is used by a variety of police and special military forces around the world.
