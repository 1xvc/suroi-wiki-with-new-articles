The **Ithaca Model 37**, also known as the **Ithaca 37** and known in-game as the **Model 37** is a pump-action shotgun utilizing the 12-gauge ammunition. It was added to the game in the v0.4.0 "Supplies recieved" update on June 17th, 2023. It has high damage, but short range and lots of recoil.
